COVID-19 Prevention Policy

The following measures are being implemented to prevent the spread of COVID-19

  • Conducting daily health checks
  • Conducting a hazard assessment of the workplace
  • Encouraging employees to wear cloth face coverings in the workplace
  • Policies and practices for social distancing in the workplace
  • Improving the building ventilation system
  • Sanitization of workplace and surroundings

TOTAL QUALITY ASSURANCE DURING COVID-19

Ringmann's first priority is always health and safety while delivering outstanding service and products to our customers. All our despatches are being santized before moving out of the premises.

Due to COVID-19, you may experience longer wait times for the products to get delivered. We apologize for the inconvenience this may cause and will continue to update you as the situation changes.
